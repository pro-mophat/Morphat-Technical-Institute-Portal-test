Annual Science Fair

May 15, 2025 Auditorium

Join us for a day filled with innovative science projects, interactive exhibits, and inspirational talks by our students and guest experts.

About the Event

The Annual Science Fair is an exciting showcase of our students' ingenuity and passion for discovery. This event encourages creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving as students present their experiments, models, and research projects. Visitors will have the opportunity to engage with interactive exhibits, participate in workshops, and learn about the latest scientific advancements.

Whether you're a student, parent, or science enthusiast, the fair offers something for everyone. Explore diverse projects, gain insights from experts, and be inspired by the next generation of innovators.
